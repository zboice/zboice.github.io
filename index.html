My personal website to share projects and simulations

The video above shows waves on a string propagating due to a point source. This code assumes that the boundaries are free, which is equivalent to imagining that we have our computational domain is just a small region of a much larger string. What's neat is that this was simulated by "compactifying" an infinite physical domain into a finite computational domain, the opposite of how we might intuitively think about solving this problem.
